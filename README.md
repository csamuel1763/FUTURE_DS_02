# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Retaining existing customers is often more cost-effective than acquiring new ones, making churn analysis essential for sustainable growth.

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior, identifying key churn drivers, and providing actionable business recommendations using Python and Power BI. The objective is to uncover patterns that influence customer retention and help businesses make data-driven decisions to reduce customer attrition.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer retention and churn behavior.
* Identify the factors contributing to customer churn.
* Understand customer lifetime patterns and tenure trends.
* Segment customers based on contract type, payment method, and internet service.
* Generate actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

### 1. Contract Type Impact

Customers with **month-to-month contracts** exhibit the highest churn rate compared to customers on long-term contracts.

### 2. Customer Tenure Impact

Most churned customers belong to the **0–1 year tenure segment**, indicating that new customers are more likely to leave.

### 3. Internet Service Impact

Customers subscribed to **fiber optic services** demonstrate higher churn compared to DSL and non-internet service users.

### 4. Payment Method Impact

Customers using **electronic check payments** are more likely to churn than customers using automated payment methods.

### 5. Pricing Impact

Customers with **higher monthly charges** show a greater tendency to discontinue services.

---

## 🎯 High-Risk Customer Profile

The analysis identified the following characteristics among customers most likely to churn:

* Month-to-Month Contract
* Less Than One Year of Tenure
* Fiber Optic Internet Service
* Electronic Check Payment Method
* Higher Monthly Charges

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Promote Long-Term Contracts

Encourage customers to switch to annual or multi-year plans through discounts and incentives.

### 2. Improve Customer Onboarding

Strengthen engagement programs during the first year of customer acquisition.

### 3. Enhance Service Quality

Investigate customer concerns related to fiber optic services and improve service experience.

### 4. Encourage Automated Payments

Promote credit card and bank transfer payment options to increase customer retention.

### 5. Launch Loyalty Programs

Reward long-term customers with exclusive benefits, discounts, and retention offers.
